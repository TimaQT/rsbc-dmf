RoadSafetyBC's Driver Portal

RoadSafetyBC is part of the Ministry of Public Safety and Solicitor General and is dedicated to maintaining road safety. Assessing the medical fitness of drivers is just one of the ways we fulfill this commitment. This portal will:

  • Allow you to see the status of your file.
  • Give you access to timely information.
  • Allow you to upload and download documents online.
  • Improve public safety and contribute to RoadSafetyBC's Vision Zero goal
